What America Buys

Using real US Census Bureau Monthly Retail Trade Survey data, this project analyzes how American retail changed from 2019 to 2023. Key findings: retail survived a 14.7% COVID crash and recovered in just 4 months, a pandemic boom added +52% growth by 2023, e-commerce accelerated 4+ years ahead of schedule, and December accounts for 30% more spending than the average month.
