# E-Commerce Acceleration During COVID

## The 5.5-Year Leap
E-commerce share of total retail:
- Q4 2019: 10.6% (pre-pandemic baseline)
- Q2 2020: 16.1% (COVID peak — one quarter later)
- **Change: +5.5 percentage points in a single quarter**

Normal adoption rate: ~1 percentage point per year
COVID compressed 5.5 years of normal adoption into 12 weeks.

## Did It Stick?
- Q4 2020: 14.2% (some normalization as stores reopened)
- Q4 2021: 13.8% (settled at permanently higher level)
- Q4 2023: 15.6% (continued steady growth)

## Conclusion
COVID created a **permanent structural shift** — e-commerce share
never returned to its pre-pandemic baseline. The pandemic effectively
pulled forward the timeline for e-commerce adoption by 3-4 years
even after the initial spike normalized.
